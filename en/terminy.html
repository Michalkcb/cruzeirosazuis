        In the 2023 season, we sail again in Greek waters: Aegean Sea - Cyclades and Sporades, Saronic Gulf, Argolic Gulf, Ionian Sea. Due to the rather unstable weather conditions in 2022, which makes it difficult to plan the cruises precisely, in 2023 we will start from Aegina. This island, the kingdom of pistachios, turned out to be the most peaceful place in the vicinity of Athens. Final sailing routes will be subject to weather conditions. From E.Vnizelosa/Athens airport take bus X96 about 1h5 to Piraeus, then ferry about 1 hour to Aegina. Fast and classic ferries from Piraeus to Aegina run every hour during the season.
        The famous Greek siga-siga (σιγα-σιγα, slow life) can be heard at every step. Certainly, we could learn from the Greeks a looser approach to life and the ability to enjoy every moment. Greek life assumes that there is no point in worrying about things that are beyond our control. Some Greeks say they were made to rest, and there is something to it! Therefore, where, if not in Greece, you can indulge in diligent lazing around! In the wonderful atmosphere of Greek tavernas, their hospitality and kindness (κεφι), you can have a great time, relax and take a break from our way of life in the constant "hurry", and moreover, the wonderful sea and islands are ideal places for sailing and other water sports. This year, in the summer, the water temperature in the sea reached 32 ° C, and at the end of October it was still 25 ° C.
       I invite you to sunny Greece!
