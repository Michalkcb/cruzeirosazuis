    >> GREECE <<


        In the 2022 season, we are sailing in GREECE because last year's plans were thwarted by the Covid-19 virus pandemic! Almost all cruises had to be canceled! Unfortunately, the state of emergency in Portugal imprisoned us in Seixal / near Lisbon until April 30, 2021, then the technical break at IATE-STORE in Amora-Seixal, led by Carlos Felisberto, lasted until 30/06/2021. All further plans to sail through the Canary Islands to Cape Verde have gone wrong, we are postponing it to the 2022/2023 season! Season 2022 we sail in Greek waters: Aegean Sea - Cyclades and Sporades, Saronic Gulf, Argolic Bay, Ionian Sea.


    yacht master Robert Bany
